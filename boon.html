How to Build a Freezer Stash WITHOUT Pumping

Using the Boon Trove to passively collect breastmilk while nursing — no pump, no cleanup, no stress.

In my first six months as a new mom, I was an exclusive breastfeeder — and I was absolutely all over the internet trying to figure out how to build a little extra freezer stash. I was Googling what time of day to pump, for how many minutes, on which side. Then I'd get completely overwhelmed just thinking about cleaning pump parts. And honestly? I was terrified of oversupplying and ending up with mastitis or a clogged duct.

Then a friend gifted me the Boon Trove — and once I tried it, I realized it was the answer to every single question I'd been stressing about.

The Method

How the Boon Trove Works

The concept is simple: while you're nursing on one side, you place the Boon Trove on the other. You can let it sit there passively to catch let-down drips, or you can press it gently against your breast with your finger to create light suction. I always went the suction route to collect more volume.

Each morning, I rotated which side the Trove went on — left on Monday, right on Tuesday, and so on — so neither side was stimulated more than the other.

The most important tip: start early. Like, first-week-home early. That's when your body is still figuring out how much milk to produce, and any consistent stimulation signals it to make a little more. Starting early is what makes this method work.

In the beginning, I was collecting about 2 oz per morning session. As my daughter grew and my supply increased, I started pulling 4 oz every single day — and 4 oz a day adds up fast. That milk became my go-to reserve whenever I wanted a night out, a drink, or just to leave her with a sitter without stress.

Storage Note

How I Store It

I stored the collected milk in the fridge in these Medela breastmilk storage bottles — my absolute favorite because the lids twist to display the day of the week. No labeling, no mess, no guessing how old the milk is.

Once you've accumulated enough, transfer it to freezer-safe bags and you've got your stash. No pump required.

This method checked every box for me: no extra time (I was already sitting there nursing anyway), no pump setup or cleanup — the Boon Trove rinses in seconds — no risk of oversupply since you're only collecting what your body naturally lets down on the opposite side, and not once did I deal with mastitis or a clogged duct.

That's it. That's the whole hack. My biggest secret — I hope it saves you as much stress as it saved me. 🤍

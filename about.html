New Mama. Full Heart. Honest Finds.

Welcome to the Steph F Edit — a space for the things I love and the things that genuinely work. Recipes for real life, pre and postpartum workouts, honest baby finds, effortless style, and everything in between.

I'm a first-time mom who believes a beautiful life is built in the ordinary moments. I'm just here to share mine, and I'm so glad you found your way here.

First Post — Hi, I'm Steph

I'm a first-time mom, and like most first-time moms, I spent an embarrassing amount of time researching everything before my daughter arrived. Some of those obsessively researched picks didn't pan out. Some things I grabbed last minute became my most-used items. That's just how it goes.

This blog is my way of putting it all in one place. Being a mom is a hard job, and becoming one made me realize just how valuable other moms' advice, finds, and honest experiences really are. I want to share what's working for me — with the full caveat that I'm still figuring it out, and probably always will be. But honestly, that's the fun of it.

Thanks for being here.

— Steph

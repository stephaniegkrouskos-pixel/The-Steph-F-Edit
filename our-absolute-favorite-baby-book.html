Our Absolute Favorite Baby Book

All Things Baby  ·  By Steph

We've read a million children's books. And somehow the one that gets me every single time is one I almost never hear mentioned: On The Night You Were Born by Nancy Tillman.

I don't know why it flies under the radar. It's so simple — just this quiet reminder to your child that the whole world noticed when they arrived. That they're one of a kind. That they matter that much.

It almost makes me cry every time I read it — especially that ending, where heaven blew every trumpet and sounded every horn on the wonderful, marvelous night you were born. Gets me every single time.

Every baby needs to hear it, and every parent needs it on their shelf. That's it. Just wanted to share.
